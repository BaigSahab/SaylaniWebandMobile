About Us

Durable Furniture is India's first online furnitures store that has been in business since April 2013 with the goal of making modern design more accessible. With over a year in business, Durable Furnitures has established a lot by offering a wide-range of well designed, functional home and office furnishing products. It has been an established and popular company with an excellent track record for best customer satisfaction. Durable Furnitures has never compromised on the quality and services provided to their customers. Customer satisfaction is their top priority, in which they believe in keeping the customers happy and providing them with products at very competent prices. Always on the look-out, we are constantly growing our catalog to offer you a wealth of fresh ideas and new finds each time you visit. We offer something for every design enthusiast, whether you're making over an entire space or refreshing the details. Every day, we work to enable you to live life more fully through design and to help you through each phase of your own style evolution. Thanks for letting us be part of your journey!

The first Durable Furnitures store opened in 2013, selling a small array of Furnitures imported from France and New Jersey. Since then, the brand has expanded to hundreds of products from around the world, more than 50 stores nationwide, a direct-mail business that distributes millions of catalogs a year, and a highly successful e-commerce site. What has never changed is Durable Furnitures dedication to customer service and strong commitment to quality.
